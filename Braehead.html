Things to do in Braehead Plenty of options for food so if you get hungry you should head on over to the food area there are many options for what you might be feeling like Zizzi’s for pizza, TGI Fridays for burgers, yo sushi for sushi and a few other places that could sort your appetite for the day. If you’re looking for more physical activities there is an ice-skating rink or a skydiving wind tunnel, there is also a rock-climbing wall for you to tackle if you’re feeling brave and finally there is go karting if you’re looking for a rush or fun. If you come at the right time there could be events on in the Braehead arena there can be events like concerts, sporting events and shows. If you’re interested in a slow stroll or a nice walk-through nature you can follow the path by the riverside.
